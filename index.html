Happy Birthday, my Annpot!

To Ann, on this day so dear,

I pen my heart, with love sincere,

Your caring ways, a constant guide,

My shelter strong, in you I confide.


Through every trial, you stand by me,

A rock so solid, a calming sea,

Your happiness, my utmost goal,

In your smile, I find my soul.


With every year that graces your path,

You bring laughter and joy that forever last,

Through ups and downs, in joy or defeat,

With you by my side, life is complete.


So let's raise a toast to the journey ahead,

Filled with moments we'll fondly spread,

May your birthday be as wonderful as you,

Happy birthday, Ann, my love so true.
